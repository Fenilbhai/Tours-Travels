Hotel Atlantis

Dubai Atlantis is a luxury resort located on the Palm Jumeirah island in Dubai, United Arab Emirates. It is one of the most iconic and popular destinations for tourists visiting Dubai. The resort is designed to offer a unique and luxurious experience to its guests, with its world-class facilities and stunning architecture

Burj Al Arab

Burj Al Arab is a luxury hotel located in Dubai, United Arab Emirates. It is considered one of the most iconic buildings in the world and has become a symbol of modern Dubai. The hotel is located on an artificial island and is designed to resemble a sailboat, with its distinctive silhouette rising 321 meters above the Arabian Gulf.

Jumeirah Brach Hotel

Dubai Jumeirah Beach Hotel is a luxury resort located on the shores of Jumeirah Beach in Dubai, United Arab Emirates. It is one of the most iconic and sought-after destinations for tourists visiting Dubai. The resort offers a unique and luxurious experience to its guests, with its stunning architecture, world-class facilities, and unparalleled hospitality. The resort features 618 guest rooms and suites, each with stunning views of the Arabian Gulf or the iconic Burj Al Arab hotel.

FIVE Palm Jumeirah Hotel

The FIVE Palm Jumeirah Hotel is a luxury hotel located on the iconic Palm Jumeirah in Dubai, United Arab Emirates. The hotel boasts a stunning beachfront location, with breathtaking views of the Arabian Gulf and Dubai's skyline. The hotel features 470 luxurious rooms and suites, each designed to offer the utmost comfort and style to guests.

Palazzo Versace Hotel

Palazzo Versace Dubai is a luxurious hotel located in the heart of the Culture Village in Dubai, United Arab Emirates. The hotel is inspired by the Italian fashion house, Versace, and features opulent decor and design throughout. The hotel offers 215 luxurious rooms and suites, each uniquely designed with Italian craftsmanship and bespoke furniture.
