Bhurj Khalifa

Burj Khalifa is a skyscraper located in the city of Dubai, United Arab Emirates, and is currently the tallest building in the world, standing at a height of 828 meters (2,716 feet). It is one of Dubai's most popular tourist attractions, drawing millions of visitors every year.

Burj Al Arab

Burj Al Arab is a luxury hotel located in Dubai, United Arab Emirates. It is considered one of the most iconic buildings in the world and has become a symbol of modern Dubai. The hotel is located on an artificial island and is designed to resemble a sailboat, with its distinctive silhouette rising 321 meters above the Arabian Gulf.

Dubai Aquarium

Dubai Aquarium & Underwater Zoo is one of the top tourist attractions in Dubai, located inside the Dubai Mall. It is one of the largest indoor aquariums in the world and houses over 140 species of marine animals, including over 300 sharks and rays. The aquarium features a 10 million-liter tank that is home to a diverse range of aquatic creatures, including Sand Tiger Sharks, Stingrays, Giant Groupers, and many more.

Miracle Garden

Dubai Miracle Garden is a stunning botanical garden that is located in the Dubailand area of Dubai. It is one of the largest flower gardens in the world and is home to over 45 million blooming flowers across 72,000 square meters of land. The garden features a vast collection of flowers and plants arranged in unique and creative patterns, including floral arches, flowerbeds, and topiary-style displays.

Dubai Fountain

The Dubai Fountain is a popular tourist attraction in the city of Dubai, United Arab Emirates. It is located at the base of the iconic Burj Khalifa, the tallest building in the world, and is considered one of the largest choreographed fountains in the world. The fountain features water jets that shoot up to 500 feet high, and is illuminated by over 6,600 lights and 25 colored projectors.

Motiongate Dubai

Dubai Motiongate is a popular theme park located in Dubai, United Arab Emirates, and is one of the key attractions for tourists visiting the city. The park is inspired by Hollywood movies and features several themed zones based on popular film franchises such as Shrek, Ghostbusters, Hunger Games, and Kung Fu Panda, among others. Visitors to Dubai Motiongate can enjoy a range of exciting rides, interactive shows, and attractions.
